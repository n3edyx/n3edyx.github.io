Who we are

Nowadays, there is less and less space in the cities. As the population keeps increasing, real estate’s prices increase too as well as the amount of people struggling to find a place to live in. That’s why we need to find a way to build cheap, stackable and sustainable buildings. Following this principle, we searched how such things could be done. With our team of engineers, we developed STACK Technologies to help people in need find affordable housing. Have you ever wondered why it’s so expensive to build a house ? No more wondering, today this is no longer the case.

No more waste

Our idea is to take waste we can recycle to make cubic areas we can live in. We need to optimize the usable space inside this cube, in order to set up basic amenagement. This is how we could reach our goal, to house homeless people.

Process

1

We gather

The process starts by negotiating with states and enterprises, our aim being that they pay us to collect the waste they produce and cannot afford to get rid of. As our purpose isn’t to get rich but to help save the planet, we are able to adapt our price to each client.

2

We recycle

After having gathered a huge amount of waste, we take it to our facilities, where the recycling process begins. The waste that fits the requirements of the compacting process is being taken to the compacting department, while the rest is analyzed and split between different partner companies depending on the use that it can still have. In the compacting department, the machines that our engineers developed take given quantities of solid waste and turn them into blocks.

3

We build

We then take our blocks and assemble them into walls, that will turn into livable houses. From there, everything is possible. Going from a single room to a whole house, everyone can design their own buildings according to both their needs and their means.

Eco-friendly

Our teams work hard to make this process possible and clean, as we want to make sure that our project is the best for the Earth.

Second life

We try to give a second life to most of the objects we collect, either by giving them to people in need or by giving them to partner companies to recycle them in the most appropriate way.

Flexible

We think that the services we offer should not be exclusive to a given range of people. That’s why everyone is able to create their own homes, or to get ones that are already available if they fit their needs.

We don't only build houses,
we build a community.

STACK Technologies aims to develop a new vision of housing: a more ecological and affordable lodgement. Habitations that would put interactions in the middle of our lives. Indeed, we do not want to create housing only. We want to create a new living environment. To understand this, you have to realize the world we live in now: are you interested in the person sitting next to you in the metro? Do your children have a setting to discover and explore the world? Is the current city life favorable to meetings ? If, like us, you think the answer is no and that the city needs to be rethought, you must discover the STACK technology. Concretely, we believe that the safety of the population, their relations and the environment are the basis of the quality of life and therefore of happiness. Our cities are too stressful and we no longer even think about the essentials: it is no longer even shocking to live with a mask all the time, to breathe polluted air all our life.

Our Solutions

1

We had to optimize as much as possible the space which is reduced in the cities with the increasing population.

2

We had to reduce the production costs of our habitats so that they are accessible to everyone.

3

We need the best designed habitats so that they require the least possible energy.

4

Our habitats must favor the collective rather than the individual.
